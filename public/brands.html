How to Spot Authentic Streetwear

  • Check for quality stitching and materials
  • Verify brand logos and tags
  • Look for serial numbers and authenticity cards
  • Research the brand's typical design patterns

Common Counterfeit Signs

  • Poor quality printing and embroidery
  • Incorrect font usage in logos
  • Missing or incorrect care labels
  • Unrealistic pricing
