üß∫ Care Instructions

Washing

  • Machine wash cold (30¬∞C/86¬∞F)
  • Use mild detergent
  • Turn garments inside out
  • Wash similar colors together

Drying

  • Air dry when possible
  • Tumble dry low if necessary
  • Remove promptly to prevent wrinkles
  • Avoid direct sunlight

Ironing

  • Iron on low heat
  • Use steam setting if available
  • Iron inside out for dark colors
  • Test on inconspicuous area first

Storage

  • Store in cool, dry place
  • Use padded hangers for delicate items
  • Keep away from direct sunlight
  • Consider garment bags for special pieces
