🧺 Care Instructions

Washing

  • Machine wash cold (30°C/86°F)
  • Use mild detergent
  • Turn garments inside out
  • Wash similar colors together

Drying

  • Air dry when possible
  • Tumble dry low if necessary
  • Remove promptly to prevent wrinkles
  • Avoid direct sunlight

Ironing

  • Iron on low heat
  • Use steam setting if available
  • Iron inside out for dark colors
  • Test on inconspicuous area first

Storage

  • Store in cool, dry place
  • Use padded hangers for delicate items
  • Keep away from direct sunlight
  • Consider garment bags for special pieces

💡 Style Tips

🎨

Color Coordination

Start with a neutral base and add neon accents. Don't be afraid to mix bold colors, but keep it to 2-3 main colors per outfit.

📐

Layering

Master the art of layering with different textures and lengths. Start with a base layer and build up with jackets, vests, or overshirts.

⚡

Accessories

Accessories can make or break an outfit. Add cyberpunk elements like LED jewelry, reflective patches, or tech-inspired bags.

👟

Footwear

Your shoes should complement your outfit's aesthetic. Chunky sneakers work well with most streetwear looks.
